VENUE - Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)


About MAHE

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is one of the institutes of Eminence in the country as announced by Got of India. It is a deemed-to-be University from 1993, Situated in Manipal, Udupi, Karnataka. It was founded in 1953 with a vision to transform society by delivering affordable quality education. There are 30 institutions/departments under the MAHE umbrella, with over 33,000 students across 23 diverse disciplines. All institutions/departments under MAHE have made a name for themselves in the education domain through its various student-friendly processes and systems. MAHE is ranked 7th in the university sector per the latest NIRF ranking by the Government of India. Manipal Centre for Natural Sciences (MCNS), a Centre of Excellence under MAHE with a vision to produce world-class researchers in the different branches of fundamental sciences. MCNS has an Astrophysics group and other branches like Nuclear Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Earth Sciences. MCNS is an active partner in developing Solar Ultraviolet Imaging Telescope (SUIT), one of the payloads onboard Aditya-L1

How to reach:

MAHE is situated in the town of Manipal, Udupi, Karnataka. Manipal is on the west coast, 60 km north of Mangalore City.

  • Nearest Airport: Mangalore Airport (IXE) ~ 50 km
  • Nearest Railway Station: Udupi (UD) ~ 3 kms, Mangalore Junction (MAJN)/ Mangalore Central (MAQ) ~50 km
  • Multi-Axle bus connectivity is available with Mumbai, Pune and Bangalore and Goa.
Tourist attractions:
  • Many famous beaches on the west coast, like Malpe, Kaup, Murudeswar
  • Western Ghats scenic locations like Augumbe, Kudremukh,
  • Well-known cultural centres like Udupi and Sringeri.
